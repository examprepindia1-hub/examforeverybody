Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 28, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to ExamForEverybody ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring your personal data is handled in compliance with applicable laws, including the Information Technology Act, 2000 (India) and global standards.

2. Information We Collect

  • Account Information: Name, email address, password, and profile photo when you register.
  • Payment Data: We do NOT store complete credit card details. Payment transactions are processed via secure gateways (UPI, PayPal, Razorpay). We only retain transaction IDs and billing addresses.
  • Usage Data: Information about courses accessed, mock test scores, time spent, and learning progress to generate your dashboard analytics.

3. How We Use Your Data

We use your information to:

  • Provide access to courses, mock tests, and workshops.
  • Process payments and generate invoices.
  • Send transactional emails (OTP, receipts, password resets).
  • Analyze platform performance to improve our educational content.

4. Data Sharing & Third Parties

We do not sell your personal data. We may share data with:

  • Instructors: Only your name and course progress are visible to instructors of courses you enroll in.
  • Service Providers: Hosting (AWS), Email (SMTP services), and Payment Processors (HDFC/PayPal) strictly for operational purposes.

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures, including SSL encryption, database hashing, and secure UPI verification protocols to protect your data.
