How can we help you?

Find answers to common questions about our mock tests, technical issues, and billing.

ExamForEverybody is a platform that helps students prepare for competitive exams through two main offerings: Mock Tests for practice and preparation, and Scholarship Tests which are competitive exams where meritorious students can win prizes to support their education.

Mock Tests are practice exams designed to help you prepare for your actual entrance exams. You can take them multiple times, review solutions, and track your progress. Many are free.

Scholarship Tests are competitive exams where students compete against each other. Top performers receive prize money or scholarships to help fund their education. These have specific dates and can only be attempted once.
