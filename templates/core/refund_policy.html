Refund & Cancellation Policy

Effective Date: December 28, 2025

1. Digital Products Policy

At ExamForEverybody, our products (Mock Tests, Courses, Study Materials) are digital and non-tangible irrevocable goods. Access is granted immediately upon purchase.

2. Refunds

We generally do not offer refunds once the product has been accessed. However, we understand exceptional circumstances may occur.

We offer a 7-day refund policy ONLY under the following conditions:

  • The accidental double payment was made for the same course/test.
  • The content is technically defective and our support team failed to resolve the issue within 48 hours.
  • You have NOT accessed or attempted more than 20% of the course content or 1 mock test.
3. Cancellation

You may cancel your account at any time via the "Danger Zone" in your profile. Cancellation does not trigger an automatic refund for previously purchased lifetime access courses.

4. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, email us at support@examforeverybody.com with:

  • Subject: Refund Request - [Order ID]
  • Reason for the refund
  • Screenshot of the issue (if applicable)
